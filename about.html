What Drives Us

Our mission & vision

Two short sentences that shape every decision we make.

Our Mission

To elevate the standard of cleaning in every home and business we touch — proving that a spotless space is not a luxury, but a foundation for clearer thinking, calmer living, and more productive work. We exist to give people their time back, and a space they're proud of.

Our Vision

To become the Rhein-Ruhr-Region's most quietly trusted cleaning studio — known not for marketing volume, but for the calibre of our crews, the rigour of our training, and the relationships we build with each client. We will never franchise. We will never compromise on the people who do the work.

What We Stand For

Five values, lived daily

These aren't posters on a wall. They're the filters we run every decision through.

Integrity

We do what we said we'd do, exactly when we said we'd do it. If we mess up, we say so — and we fix it, fast, no excuses.

Craft

Cleaning is not a chore to us — it's a craft. We study tools, techniques, and chemistry so you don't have to think about any of it.

Care

We treat every space as if it were our own. Your home, your office, your restaurant — it matters to us because it matters to you.

Reliability

You'll never have to wonder if we're showing up. Same crew, same time, same spotless result — visit after visit, year after year.

Sustainability

Plant-based products, refillable bottles, electric vehicles. We obsess over our environmental footprint as much as your dust bunnies.

People First

Our crews are employees, not contractors. They earn a living wage, get healthcare, and stay with us for years. You meet the same faces.

Our Journey

Fifteen years, one obsession

From a single Düsseldorfer Wohnung to the Rhein-Ruhr-Region's most quietly trusted cleaning studio.

2010

The first apartment

Founder Amélie Laurent starts BlitzBlank in a 400 sq ft Düsseldorfer Altstadt-Studio, cleaning for neighbours on weekends with a borrowed vacuum and a single bucket of plant-based products.

2013

First commercial client

Northwind Studio signs on as our first recurring office contract — and is still a client today. We hire our first three full-time crew members.

2016

The Königsallee-Zentrale

We move into a converted warehouse on Königsallee, build a formal training program, and adopt our 80-hour onboarding curriculum for every new hire.

2019

Medical & industrial divisions

Specialised crews trained to OSHA, HIPAA, and CDC standards launch. We pass every audit on first inspection — a streak we still hold today.

2022

All-electric fleet

The last combustion van retires. Every vehicle in our fleet is electric. Carbon-neutral certification achieved through combination of EVs, solar-powered HQ, and verified offsets.

2025

500 clients & counting

A milestone we celebrated quietly with a team dinner. Same mission, same standards, same refusal to franchise. The next decade starts tomorrow.
